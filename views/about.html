Who Are We?

Founded in 2009, Las Pinas Persons with Disability Federation Inc. (LPPWDFI) is dedicated to uniting and empowering individuals with disabilities within Las Pinas City.

Our mission is to foster self-reliance and productivity among our members, ensuring they can actively contribute to and participate in society. We believe in collaboration, innovation, and inclusivity.

Mission

To unite persons with disabilities in Las Pinas City to be a model federation, empowering persons with disabilities to become self-reliant and productive members of society.

Vision

To be a federation of empowered persons with disabilities working towards promoting an all-inclusive nation.
